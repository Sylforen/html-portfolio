The Best Movies According to Austin

Some of my favourite movies of all-time.

In no particular order:

    Lord of the Rings

    Masterpiece. The Hobbit didn't need three films.

    Deadpool

    Insanity from start to finish. The sequel didn't really expand upon the formula.

    The Green Mile

    Beautiful. For when you need to cry.

    Studio Ghibli

    Shout outs in particular to Howl's Moving Castle, Spirited Away, and Totoro.
